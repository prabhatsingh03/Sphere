Purchase Order Approval Hierarchy

Discipline Amount Range Requestor First Approver Second Approver Workflow
Engineering < ₹10 Lakh HPPC, Head of Engineering Head of Procurement CFO Two-stage approval required
Engineering ₹10 Lakh - ₹10 Crore Head of Engineering, CFO CEO None Single-stage approval
Engineering ≥ ₹10 Crore CEO, CFO Chairman None Single-stage approval
Procurement < ₹25 Lakh HPPC, Site Head Mechanical Head of Procurement CFO Two-stage approval required
Procurement ≥ ₹25 Lakh Head of Procurement, CFO CEO None Single-stage approval
Construction < ₹1 Lakh HPPC, Head of Engineering Head of Procurement None Single-stage approval
Construction ₹1 Lakh - ₹10 Lakh HPPC, Head of Engineering Head of Procurement CFO Two-stage approval required
Construction ₹10 Lakh - ₹25 Crore CFO, Head of Procurement CEO None Single-stage approval
Construction ≥ ₹25 Crore CFO, CEO Chairman None Single-stage approval

User Permissions & Roles

Admin Role

  • Full system access
  • User management & configuration
  • All approval permissions
  • System configuration

Regular User Permissions

  • Purchase Requisition creation
  • Purchase Order generation
  • Status checking & tracking
  • Document upload & retrieval

Complete Workflow Process

1

Create PR

User creates Purchase Requisition with project details, items, and budget

2

Generate PO

System generates Purchase Order based on PR and sends to first approver

3

First Approval

Head of Procurement reviews and approves/rejects the PO

4

Final Approval

CFO provides final approval (if required) and PO is ready for execution

Key System Features

Email Notifications

Automatic email notifications at every stage with direct approval/rejection links

Real-time Tracking

Live dashboard with charts, statistics, and real-time status updates

Role-based Access

Secure access control with granular permissions for different user roles
